## A good decision starts with the right information.

Every process in Croatia has administrative, financial and practical steps. Before committing, define your goal, budget and timeline, and confirm which professionals need to be involved.

## How to approach the process

Start with an initial consultation, prepare the documents you already have and avoid relying on unverified information. Local knowledge helps identify risk before it becomes an expensive problem.
